General Motivation

At this point my motivation to do my job and to look for a new one in the Netherlands is storng will to immigrate, and what is more important, my desire to participate in development of products which aim to make peoples life better using power of design and technology.

My work experince is all about finding the way I could be more useful and inspired. I had started with nursing at city hospital, when my dream was to became addiction psychiatrists. Being high school student I switched to fashion design. Then I got my associates degree in it and worked at fashion design studio, where I realized my need in consistency in design and passion to technology.

After I left my job in fashion design I started career of web and graphic designer on freelance and moved on. Working on different task with different people helped me to develop the way I could understand other's and explain my own thoughts better. By now I have experience in both web and mobile design for business and entertainment. I developd very practical understanding of design and management tools and practices. I'm permanently mastering my knowledge in wide range of fields.

While working in teams with great professionals I learned a lot about front-end development and practicies in it. Practiced it from small fixes to landings with simple interactions designed in browser.

I also gain a lot of experience in project management, which is important for me as an UI/UX designer, because both of those specialisations involves deep understanding of project and effective communication with team.
